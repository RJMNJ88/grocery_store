a fresh start

Here at Fresh Co-op, we believe in a less-is-more approach to farming. Without the use of chemicals, hormones, or anti-biotics, our members produce the most nutritious, environmentally friendly food you can buy.

what is a co-op ?

A Co-op is a member-owned and operated company that is run exclusively for the benefit of its members. All revenue is put back into our operation, and all profit is used to reduce the cost of food in our stores.

become a member

From store clerk to farm hand, there are so many ways to contribute. Of course, if you're just looking for a great place to buy quality groceries, that's cool too !
